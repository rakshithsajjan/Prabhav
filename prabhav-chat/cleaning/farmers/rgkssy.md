=========================================

Accident

Farmer

Financial Support

[### Details](https://www.myscheme.gov.in/schemes/rgkssy#details)

The Rajasthan government's Rajiv Gandhi Krishak Sathi Sahayta Yojana is a critical safety net for those who keep our food system running smoothly. Farmers, farmworkers, and market staff face inherent risks in their daily activities. This program acknowledges those risks and provides much-needed financial assistance in times of crisis. If an accident occurs while they're tending to crops, raising livestock, or selling their produce at market, the Yojana can help soften the financial blow. This can mean the difference between a family being able to afford medical care and essential needs, or facing devastating hardship.

[### Eligibility](https://www.myscheme.gov.in/schemes/rgkssy#eligibility)

1.  The Farmers/agricultural laborers of the state.
2.  The workers hold licenses from the market committee.

[### Application Process](https://www.myscheme.gov.in/schemes/rgkssy#application-process)

Online

> **Registration**

**Step-1:** Applicant have to visit the [official portal](https://sso.rajasthan.gov.in/signin).

**Step-2:** Click on the option “[Register](https://sso.rajasthan.gov.in/register)”.

**Step-3:** Then you will be redirected to the SSO registration page. The registration page will appear with the following options.

*   Citizen

**Step-4:** Choose the either one option from the Jan Aadhaar Or Google to process further.

*   Jan Aadhaar : Enter the Jan Aadhaar number, click on the ‘Next’ button,

*   Select your name, the name of the head of the family and all the other members and
*   Click on the ‘Send OTP’ button. Enter the ‘OTP’ and
*   Click on the ‘Verify OTP’ button to Complete the registration.

*   Google : Enter the Gmail ID, click on the ‘Next’ button,

*   Enter the password.
*   A new link appear on screen, now click on the new SSO link.
*   SSO id will appear on the screen, now create the password.
*   Enter Mobile number, click on registration.

**Step-5**: Submit.

﻿

> **Apply**

**Step-1:** Applicant have to visit the [official portal](https://sso.rajasthan.gov.in/signin).

**Step-2:** After login, [dashboard](https://sso.rajasthan.gov.in/dashboard) will open.

**Step-3:** Click on “ RAJ-KISAN ” option.

**Step-4:** In "Farmer", click on "Application Entry Request".

**Step-5:** Enter the "Bhamashah ID" or "Janaadhaar ID" and search.

**Step-6:** Select the person name and scheme name.

**Step-7:** Complete the Aadhaar Authentication and click on Get details.

**Step-8:** Provide the required details.

\- Pensioner Details.

\- Bank Details.

\- Disability Details.

\- Verification Details.

\- Upload Documents.

**Step-9:** Submit.

[### Documents Required](https://www.myscheme.gov.in/schemes/rgkssy#documents-required)

1.  A medical officer's certificate required
2.  FIR Report
3.  Death Report
4.  Post-mortem report
5.  Certificate from the government doctor in case of a poisonous animal bite or camel bite.
6.  Provision for payment to be made on the Panchnama also under the signature of the Government Doctor.

[### Frequently Asked Questions](https://www.myscheme.gov.in/schemes/rgkssy#faqs)

What is the Rajiv Gandhi Krishak Sathi Sahayta Yojana?

The Rajiv Gandhi Krishak Sathi Sahayta Yojana is a scheme initiated by the Rajasthan Government to provide financial assistance to farmers and agricultural workers in case of accidental dismemberment or death while engaged in agricultural activities.

Who is eligible to benefit from this scheme?

Farmers, agricultural laborers, and market workers in Rajasthan are eligible beneficiaries under this scheme.

What type of incidents does the scheme cover?

The scheme covers accidental dismemberment or death that occurs while individuals are involved in agricultural or agricultural marketing work.

Are there any documentation requirements to avail of the benefits?

A medical officer's certificate required FIR Report Death Report post-mortem report Certificate from the government doctor in case of a poisonous animal bite or camel bite. Provision for payment to be made on the Panchnama also under the signature of the Government Doctor.

What is the eligibility criteria ?

Farmers/agricultural laborers of the state and workers holding licenses from the market committee.

What is official website for registration and apply ?

Applicants need to visit the following link. URL : https://sso.rajasthan.gov.in/signin

How much benefit will be given to the dependent on death?

₹ 2,00,000/-

Can only people of Rajasthan avail the benefits of this scheme?

Yes, only the people of Rajasthan avail the benefits.
