Baristha Bunakar Sahayata Yojana
================================

Baristha Bunakar

Financial Assistance

Sahayata Yojana

[### Details](https://www.myscheme.gov.in/schemes/bbsy#details)

**Details**

Odisha is famous for its handloom products with intricate IKAT designs, popularly known as bandha (tie-dye) designs. The handloom weaving in the State is part of its great cultural heritage and the artistry of the weavers is transferred from generation to generation which has kept the tradition alive amidst fast mechanization. Hand-woven fabric produced in different parts of Odisha draws inspiration from nature and the strength lies in innovative and intricate designs woven/made by the weavers of the State.

﻿

This scheme aims to provide financial assistance to the weavers in the state. Odisha Government has initiated various welfare schemes for the weaver community in the state and increased the reach of Handloom.

﻿

**Disbursement**

1.  After sanction is accorded, funds will be transferred directly by the Directorate to the bank account of the beneficiary.
2.  The assistance due to each beneficiary shall be disbursed through NEFT/RTGS funds transfer on monthly basis by 20th of each succeeding month.
3.  The list of beneficiaries and the guidelines shall be displayed on the Department website and in the Office of the Zonal Officer for information of the general public.

[### Benefits](https://www.myscheme.gov.in/schemes/bbsy#benefits)

**The present rate of benefits:**

*   Rs.500/- per month from aged 60 years to 79 years;
*   Rs.700/- per month from aged 80 years to above;

[### Eligibility](https://www.myscheme.gov.in/schemes/bbsy#eligibility)

**A person shall be eligible to get benefits under this Scheme if he/she:**

1.  Applicants should be 60 years of age and above
2.  Applicant must have worked at least the preceding 20 years in the respective profession (at the time of attaining 60 years).
3.  Bunakars and full-time ancillary workers will be eligible. or, is a widow (irrespective of age, subject to the condition that her husband had been a bunker / full-time ancillary worker for at least 10 years at the time of death).
4.  Applicant's family income from all sources does not exceed Rs. 1,00,000/- per annum (Tahsildar concerned to certify).
5.  Applicants are permanent residents/domiciles of Odisha.
6.  Both the Bunakar and ancillary workers should have valid weaver's identity cards issued in his/her name by the State / Central Government.
7.  The applicant should furnish an affidavit or self-declaration that he/she is not in receipt of any other monthly financial assistance/pension from the State or Central Government.
8.  The applicant should furnish an affidavit or self-declaration that he/ she has not been convicted of any criminal offense, provided that the Government may, having regard to the circumstances of each case, waive this disability, if, in their judgment, the conviction was based on the ground other than moral turpitude.
9.  Where more than one member of a family qualifies the eligibility, only one of them will be entitled to benefit under this scheme.

[### Application Process](https://www.myscheme.gov.in/schemes/bbsy#application-process)

Offline

*   **Application Form**: Application in form may be obtained from the Zonal Office of the Directorate of Textiles working for the Handloom sector or from the website of the Department of Handlooms, Textiles & Handicrafts.
*   **Submission of application:** Application in the prescribed form filled correctly in duplicate along with the documents (enclosed) shall be submitted to the Zonal Officer (DDT/ADT as the case may be). Zonal Officer will then make necessary entries of the particulars in a register duly maintained at his/her level in order of priority based on the date of receipt of the application. Such priority must be maintained in sanctioning of assistance under BBSY.

[### Documents Required](https://www.myscheme.gov.in/schemes/bbsy#documents-required)

**List of documents to be submitted along the form:—**

1.  Three identical self-attested copies of recent passport-size colour photographs.
2.  Self-attested copy of the Weavers Identity Card.
3.  Self-attested xerox copy of the 1st page of the individual Savings Bank Pass Book showing photograph, full name, name of father/spouse, address, bank, branch, account number, IFSC and MICR code details into which the assistance will be deposited. Account must be in the name of the applicant as submitted in the application form. Account must be opened in a bank with a core banking facility to enable the digital transfer of assistance.
4.  Certificate regarding total annual family income from the concerned Tahasildar.
5.  Proof of age.
6.  An affidavit that he/she is not in receipt of any such assistance from either the State or Central Government.

﻿

**Proof of Age:** The age of the applicant mentioned in the application is to be verified from any one of the following categories:

1.  Electoral Photo Identity Card.
2.  AADHAAR Card
3.  High School Certificate
4.  Birth Registration Certificate.
