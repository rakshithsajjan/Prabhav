Braille Watches by Welfare of Differently Abled Persons Department
==================================================================

Braille Watches

Differently Abled Person

Employee

Social Welfare

Visually Challenged Person

[### Details](https://www.myscheme.gov.in/schemes/bwbwodapd#details)

The 'Braille Watches by Welfare of Differently Abled Persons Department' scheme was launched by the Department for the Welfare of Differently Abled Persons, Government of Tamil Nadu. Under this scheme, braille watches are distributed to working visually impaired persons to enable them to reach the workplace on time. The applications are accepted offline in the prescribed form available at the District Differently Abled Welfare Office.

[### Benefits](https://www.myscheme.gov.in/schemes/bwbwodapd#benefits)

*   **Free distribution of braille watches.**

[### Eligibility](https://www.myscheme.gov.in/schemes/bwbwodapd#eligibility)

1.  The applicant should be a visually impaired working person.
2.  The applicant should be working in the unorganized sectors.
3.  The applicant should be any one of the following:

*   Employee.
*   Self-Employed.

[### Application Process](https://www.myscheme.gov.in/schemes/bwbwodapd#application-process)

Offline

**Step 1:** The interested applicant should visit (during office hours) the District Differently Abled Welfare Officer and request a hard copy of the prescribed format of the application form from the staff exclusively entrusted to issue and collect filled-in applications.

**Step 2:** In the application form, fill in all the mandatory fields, and attach copies of all the mandatory documents (self-attest, if required).

**Step 3:** Submit the duly filled and signed application form along with the documents to the District Differently Abled Welfare Officer.

**Step 4:** Request a receipt or acknowledgment from the District Differently Abled Welfare Officer to whom the application has been submitted. Ensure that the receipt contains essential details such as the date and time of submission, and a unique identification number (if applicable).

[### Documents Required](https://www.myscheme.gov.in/schemes/bwbwodapd#documents-required)

*   National Identity Card of the Differently Abled Person.

*   Any Other Documents If Required.

[### Frequently Asked Questions](https://www.myscheme.gov.in/schemes/bwbwodapd#faqs)

How do Braille watches help visually impaired people get to work on time?

Braille watches help visually impaired individuals reach their workplace on time by allowing them to tell time through touch.

Who can get Braille watches?

Visually impaired individuals who are either self-employed or work in the unorganized sectors are eligible to receive Braille watches.

Are Braille watches only for people who work for themselves?

Braille watches are not exclusively for self-employed individuals; they are also available for those working in small businesses or unorganized sectors.

Can people working in small businesses get Braille watches?

Yes, individuals employed in small businesses or unorganized sectors are eligible to receive Braille watches.

Do I have to pay anything for Braille watches?

There is no cost associated with obtaining Braille watches through this scheme.

What happens if Braille watch breaks?

If a Braille watch breaks, individuals can request a replacement or repair through the application process.

Can I get more than one Braille watch?

The number of Braille watches one can receive may be limited according to the scheme's guidelines.

Where can I get the application form for Braille watches?

The application form for Braille watches can be obtained from the concerned District Differently Abled Welfare Officer.

What documents do I need to apply for Braille watches?

Applicants need to provide a national identity card for differently abled individuals as a certification.

Who do I give the application to?

The application for Braille watches should be submitted to the District Differently Abled Welfare Officer.

Where do I complain if have a problem?

Grievances regarding Braille watches can be addressed to the Technical Officer at the Commissionerate for Welfare of the Differently Abled in Chennai.
