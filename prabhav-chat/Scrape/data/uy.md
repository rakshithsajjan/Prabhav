Udita Yojana
------------

[### Details](https://www.myscheme.gov.in/schemes/uy#details)

The Project Udita program was started on November 1, 2016, under the Women and Child Development Projects of Madhya Pradesh Government. According to the concept of this scheme, Anganwadi workers and assistants will inform adolescent girls about the importance of proper hygiene during menstruation and sexually transmitted diseases due to negligence and provide them with sanitary napkins. If girls become aware of their health in their teenage years, then they all can remain healthy in future also.

[### Benefits](https://www.myscheme.gov.in/schemes/uy#benefits)

*   Distribution of sanitary napkins, nutrition and health education, training on menstrual hygiene and nutrition and health issues in adolescent girls are done through Udita Corner established in Anganwadi centers.

[### Eligibility](https://www.myscheme.gov.in/schemes/uy#eligibility)

*   All adolescent girls and women aged 18 to 49 will be beneficiaries.

[### Application Process](https://www.myscheme.gov.in/schemes/uy#application-process)

*   Beneficiaries should contact Udita Corner established in Anganwadi centres.

[### Documents Required](https://www.myscheme.gov.in/schemes/uy#documents-required)

No documents are required for this scheme.

[### Frequently Asked Questions](https://www.myscheme.gov.in/schemes/uy#faqs)

What is the objective of the scheme?

Madhya Pradesh State Government started the "Udita Yojana" for women's health promotions.

Who will get the benefits ?

All adolescent girls and women.

What are the benefits undet the scheme ?

Distribution of sanitary napkins, nutrition and health education, training on menstrual hygiene and nutrition and health issues.

What Is Family Annual Income Eligibility Criteria ?

There Are No Such Criteria.

Is Unmarried woman also eligible ?

Yes, they are eligible

What is the eligible age for women?

All Women From The Age Group Of 18 To 49 Are Eligible.

What Is The Application Process?

There Is No Application Process.

What Documents Are Required ?

No Document Is Required For This Scheme.
