Equipment

Grant

Sports

[### Details](https://www.myscheme.gov.in/schemes/hposec#details)

"Haryana Provision of Sports Equipment Scheme" by the Sports Department, Government of Haryana provides sports equipment to Gram Panchayats and Municipal bodies to encourage youngsters in the state towards sports such as Volleyball, Football, Basketball, Handball, Boxing, Wrestling, Judo, Cricket. To receive the benefits, proper Field of Play (FoP) for the requisite sport should be available in the town/ village.

[### Eligibility](https://www.myscheme.gov.in/schemes/hposec#eligibility)

1.  The applicant should be a Municipal Body/Gram Panchayat of Haryana.
2.  Field of Play (FOP) should be available in the town/village for the expected play.

[### Application Process](https://www.myscheme.gov.in/schemes/hposec#application-process)

Offline

**Step 1:** The interested applicant should take print of the [prescribed format](https://cdnbbsr.s3waas.gov.in/s3e069ea4c9c233d36ff9c7f329bc08ff1/uploads/2023/09/20230919191172889.pdf) of the application form.

**Step 2:** In the application form, fill in all the mandatory fields, paste the latest photographs of the Field of Play, mention the date & location on the photograph, and attach copies of all the mandatory documents.

**Step 3:** Submit the duly filled and signed application form along with the documents to the District Sports Officer.

**Step 4:** Request a receipt or acknowledgement District Sports Officer. Ensure that the receipt contains essential details such as the date and time of submission, and a unique identification number (if applicable).

﻿

**NOTE 1:** In the case of Municipal Body application will be submitted by the concerned ward member/counsellor.

**NOTE 2:** In the case of Gram Panchayat, the Sarpanch of the village may apply. In case of a vacancy for the post of Sarpanch, Gram Sachiv may apply.

[### Documents Required](https://www.myscheme.gov.in/schemes/hposec#documents-required)

Latest photographs of the Field of Play, mentioning the date & location on the photograph.

[### Frequently Asked Questions](https://www.myscheme.gov.in/schemes/hposec#faqs)

What role does the scheme envision for local governance bodies in facilitating sports development?

Local governance bodies play a crucial role in implementing the scheme at the grassroots level, ensuring that sports infrastructure and opportunities are accessible and well-utilized within their respective jurisdictions.

How does the scheme ensure accountability and transparency in the application process?

Applicants receive a receipt upon submission of their application, which includes essential details such as the date, time of submission, and a unique identification number (if applicable). This ensures proper tracking and accountability throughout the process.

Are there any special considerations or conditions attached to the application process?

Yes, indeed. Depending on whether the application is from a Municipal Body or a Gram Panchayat, different authorities are responsible for submission. Ward members or councillors handle Municipal Body applications, while Sarpanchs or Gram Sachivs are involved for Gram Panchayats.

In terms of sports equipment provision, could you provide an overview of the support offered for individual sports?

Certainly. The scheme provides specific equipment for each sport, ranging from balls and nets for Volleyball and Football to mats for Wrestling and Judo, and comprehensive kits for Cricket.

What provisions are made for documentation, and what specific requirements are expected from applicants?

Applicants need to provide recent photographs of the Field of Play, clearly indicating the date and location. These photographs serve as crucial documentation for assessing the suitability of the playing area.

How does one go about applying for the scheme, and what are the key steps involved?

The application process involves several steps. Firstly, interested applicants need to obtain the prescribed application form and fill it out completely. Then, they must attach the required documents, including photographs of the Field of Play, and submit the application to the District Sports Officer. Finally, they should request a receipt for acknowledgment of their submission.

What range of sports are covered under this scheme, and how does it intend to promote them?

The scheme encompasses various sports such as Volleyball, Football, Basketball, Handball, Boxing, Wrestling, Judo, and Cricket. By providing equipment for these sports, it encourages active participation and development within communities.

Could you elaborate on the purpose behind the "Haryana Provision of Sports Equipment Scheme" initiated by the Sports Department, Government of Haryana?

Certainly. The scheme aims to foster sports participation among the youth by providing necessary equipment to Gram Panchayats and Municipal bodies across the state.

What specific equipment is provided for Volleyball under the scheme?

Up to 6 balls and a net (if poles are installed) are provided for Volleyball.

Describe the equipment provision for Football.

Football beneficiaries receive up to 6 balls and a net (if poles are installed).

What equipment is allocated for Basketball?

Basketball beneficiaries receive up to 6 balls (if poles are installed).

What equipment does the Boxing category entail?

Boxing beneficiaries receive up to 6 punching bags and 12 pairs of gloves.
